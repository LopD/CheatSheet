Mere uspešnosti klasifikatora.

  • $ precision = \frac{TP}{TP+FP} $
  • $ accuracy = \frac{TP+TN}{TP+TN+FP+FN} $
  • $ sensitivity = \frac{TP}{TP+FN} $
  • $ specificity = \frac{TN}{TN+FP} $
  • $ F\_score = \frac{2*precision*sensitivity}{precision+sensitivity} $
